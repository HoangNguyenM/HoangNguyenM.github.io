Biography

I am currently a PhD Candidate at Florida State University, studying and researching Mathematics. During my graduate program, I have developed my passion for Machine Learning and AI, especially in developing new theoretical results and novel algorithms. With the enthusiasm for Mathematics since I was in elementary school, I have been enriching my exceptional skills in critical thinking and problem solving. I love to work in the fields of Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning Applications, Data Science, and the related quantitative areas.

I received B.S. degree in Mathematics and B.B.A. degree in Finance, Summa Cum Laude, from The University of Mississippi in 2019. I was also an Honors Scholar with a research thesis in Portfolio Optimization methods .
