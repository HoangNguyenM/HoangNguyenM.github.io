President of the International & American Student Alliance IASA

I was an executive member of the IASA, the largest organization for international students in The University of Mississippi from 2017 - 2019, of which I became the president in 2018.

Some of my achievements in this organization include:
Members expansion: Expanded the three-year old organization into the largest international community on campus with a board of 17 directors and approximately 1000 members at University of Mississippi.
Funding: Obtained at least $2000 every semester to facilitate substantial events and various programs to provide the international students with academic support and experience.
Events: I have organized many events for the organization, including Ole Miss International Fest and Culture Night.

2016

Rank 298th scorer, Putnam Competition

I was the 298th scorer in the William Lowell Putnam Mathematical Competition 2016. Putnam competition is an annual mathematics competition for undergraduate college students enrolled at institutions in the United States and Canada. It is widely considered to be the most prestigious university-level mathematics competition in the world. Participants in the competition consist of the most talented students specializing in mathematics. More information about the competition can be found at Wikipedia - Putnam Competition.
